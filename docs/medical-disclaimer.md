# Medical Disclaimer

This project and skill are for informational review of the user's available
HealthEx-connected records. They are not medical advice, are not a substitute
for individualized clinical judgment, and may be incomplete if records from
pharmacies, travel clinics, military systems, or outside health systems are not
present in HealthEx. Users should confirm immunization decisions, missing
history, contraindications, and next steps with a qualified clinician.
